'Digitized the Gargen of Eden' : Unity Neon Challenge

Team DF LAB - 3 members
Role Total Plan & Unity Timeline & Unity Rendering
Period 2 Weeks
Tools Unity & Autodesk 3Ds max

'Unity neon challenge' was held in 2018. 'Digitized the garden of Eden' was created to submit for this competition and used the Timeline, Postprocessing and Rendering functions in Unity Editor. Literally, It shows a couple of scenes that a harmony between nature and future devices. We made some models using Maya and illustrated natural simulations using Unity particle system.

'Unity Neon Challenge'는 2018년 개최된 유니티 영상 공모전이며 DF Lab에서 제작물을 제출하였다. 유니티의 타임라인과 포스트 프로세싱, 랜더링 기능을 사용했으며 Maya를 이용하여 모델링하고 Unity Particle System으로 자연 현상 시뮬레이션을 구현하였다.
