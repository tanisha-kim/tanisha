'VR Game Centre' : VR Sports games

Team DF LAB - 3 members & REAL GAM - 2 members
Role Total Plan & UX Design & GUI Design & Rendering the Menu scene
Period 3 Months
Tools Unity, Adobe Illustrator, Autodesk 3Ds Max

This project called 'VR GAME CENTRE' is created as a social VR content in Ajou university collaboration with REAL GAM. It has a Bowling game and a Shooting game and made up with low-polygon modeling and vivid colours.

'VR Game Centre'은 아주대학교 DF lab과 (주)리얼감의 산학협력 프로젝트로 소셜VR 콘텐츠로 제작되었다. 볼링게임, 슈팅게임으로 구성되었으며 로우폴리곤 모델과 비비드한 컬러로 활동감을 더했다.
